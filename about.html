Hey! My name is Iris, I'm a junior in high school. My hobbies include swimming, video games, and hanging out with my friends.

I always loved taking photos but developed a strong interest in it during quarantine. I took a masterclass and got my first camera in late 2020. As everything slowly opened up in 2021, I started taking my camera everywhere. I went to gardens, parks, and cities and took pictures of everything and anything.

In the future, I hope to gain more experience through cinematography and product shoots.

If you would like contact me, please message me on Instagram @irisphotos.jpeg.
